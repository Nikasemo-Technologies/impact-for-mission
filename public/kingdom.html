Kingdom Partnership

Become a Kingdom Partner

At Christian Therapeutic Ministry, we believe the Great Commission cannot be fulfilled alone—we must work together as the Body of Christ (1 Corinthians 12:12–27).

That’s why we partner with churches, businesses, and health organizations to bring healing, hope, and transformation through professional spiritual care and holistic training.

What We Offer Our Partners

As a Kingdom Partner, your ministry or organization will receive access to professional services led by board-certified chaplains:

  • Pastoral and Spiritual Counseling for your members or staff
  • Scheduled Holistic Health Webinars and Trainings
  • Resiliency and Emotional Wellness Training for leaders and teams
  • Evangelism and Discipleship Training
  • Ministry Consulting and Leadership Development

Invest in a Greater Mission

By becoming a Kingdom Partner, you are not just receiving expert care—you are also investing in our Orphan Care Mission in Togo and Ghana , helping vulnerable children receive the love, stability, and future they deserve through family-based support.

Let Us Build Together

We invite you to collaborate with us—strengthen your community while transforming lives across Africa.
