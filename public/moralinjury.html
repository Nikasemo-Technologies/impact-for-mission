Moral Injury/PTSD/Mental Health Spirituality Group

This program addresses the emotional and spiritual challenges faced by individuals dealing with moral injury, PTSD, and mental health issues. Led by a board-certified chaplain, the sessions offer a safe and supportive environment for healing.

Program details

  • Confession: Provides a non-judgmental space to express moral struggles and find acceptance.
  • Forgiveness and Self-Forgiveness: Helps participants release guilt and embrace spiritual and emotional freedom.
  • Gratitude Practice: Cultivates a thankful mindset to promote healing and reframe perspectives.
  • Self-Compassion: Encourages understanding and kindness toward oneself during emotional challenges.
  • Meaning-Making: Assists participants in finding purpose and significance in their experiences.
  • Building Resiliency: Teaches strategies to develop emotional and spiritual resilience for long-term growth.

Join Us

Sessions are conducted weekly, providing participants with the tools they need to overcome struggles and rebuild their lives.
