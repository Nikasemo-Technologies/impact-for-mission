Morning/Daily Impact is a transformative 20-minute devotional experience designed to inspire and equip you for the day ahead. With uplifting words of exhortation and heartfelt prayers, it provides the spiritual nourishment you need to face the day with confidence and purpose. This session helps you align your focus on God, find strength in His Word, and start your day on the right note, regardless of life’s challenges. Join us and experience the difference it can make in your daily journey.

The “Thrive and Pray: Holistic Health Webinar” is a transformative one-hour session held every Thursday from 8:00 PM to 9:00 PM. This webinar combines biblically-based teachings with evidence-backed mental and psychological principles, offering a holistic model for therapeutic support. The final 25 minutes are dedicated to holistic healing through prayer, meditation, and spiritual renewal.

You’ll gain valuable insights on managing life’s challenges, improving mental and emotional health, and fostering spiritual growth, all while integrating your Christian faith. Join us and discover practical tools to thrive in every aspect of your life. Don’t miss this opportunity to grow, heal, and find renewal for your spirit, mind, and body.

Faith and Fellowship Hour is a non-denominational gathering for spiritual growth, held every Sunday from 8:00 AM to 9:00 AM, open to both our members and the general public. This time of connection feels like attending worship in a hospital chapel, prison facility, military base, or university campus—where inclusivity and simplicity foster a deep sense of community. It’s a unique opportunity to engage in worship, prayer, and biblical teaching, all within an environment that welcomes everyone regardless of background. Come and be part of this uplifting hour where faith is strengthened, hearts are encouraged, and lives are transformed.
