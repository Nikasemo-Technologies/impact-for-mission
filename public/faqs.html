Christian Therapeutic Ministry forms partnerships with churches, ministries, businesses, and organizations. In return for providing chaplaincy care, resiliency training, holistic health education, and ministry consulting, our partners commit to financially supporting our Orphan Care Mission in Togo and Ghana. Together, we work to advance God’s Kingdom through service, healing, and compassion.

Vision: To see every vulnerable child raised in a safe, loving, and Christ-centered family environment.

Mission: To provide holistic support—educational, medical, emotional, and spiritual—to orphans and at-risk children through family-based care models in Ghana and Togo.

To become a Kingdom Partner, contact us through our website or email. We’ll schedule a meeting to explore how we can support your organization through spiritual care and training, and how you can support our Orphan Care Mission through financial or in-kind contributions.

You can sponsor a child by completing our sponsorship form online or contacting our office. Sponsors receive a profile of the child and regular updates. Your support helps provide food, school fees, clothing, medical care, and spiritual mentoring.

Family-based care places children with relatives or trusted caregivers within their community, instead of institutions. Studies show this model offers better emotional, psychological, and spiritual outcomes. We support family-based care because it aligns with biblical values and promotes long-term well-being and identity development.
