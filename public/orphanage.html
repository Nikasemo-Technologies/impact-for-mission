Christian Therapeutic Orphanage and Hope Mission

We believe that every child deserves love, stability, and the opportunity to thrive. Rooted in Christian faith, our mission is to bring hope and restoration to orphans and underprivileged children through both residential care and family/kinship-based support.

We are led by a passionate team from Togo, Ghana, and Cameroon—committed to restoring broken childhoods and raising a new generation of hope-filled leaders.

Nurturing Homes

A Christian orphanage in Lomé, Togo, and family-based care in local communities provide a loving environment that promotes dignity and purpose.

Holistic Support

We provide full educational support, medical assistance, and vital emotional healing to ensure children can flourish physically and mentally.

Spiritual Discipleship

Our core mission is to raise children in the Christian faith, offering spiritual discipleship that builds a foundation of hope and love.

Why Family-Based Care?

Research shows that nearly 80% of children in orphanages have at least one living parent. Children raised in family-based settings flourish emotionally, socially, and spiritually.

Through our Continuum of Care Model , we evaluate each child's needs to provide the most appropriate, compassionate care—whether through our orphanage or trusted family placements.

Our Services Include:
  • Foster/Kinship Care Placement
  • Christian-based Counseling and Emotional Healing
  • Educational Sponsorship
  • Spiritual Nurture and Discipleship
