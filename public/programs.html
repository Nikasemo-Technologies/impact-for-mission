Our Programs

Discover the transformative power of faith and science. Join our mission to inspire purpose and achieve balance in all aspects of life.

Programs

Our programs focus on holistic growth and healing through faith-based initiatives. The Train-Up a Child Program nurtures Christlike values in youth, while the Moral Injury/PTSD Spirituality Group provides support for emotional and spiritual challenges. The Holistic Health Webinar promotes balance and well-being, and our Community Engagement Initiatives foster connections and service to underprivileged communities, embodying God’s love.

Train-Up a Child Program
The Train-Up a Child Program is a Bible-based initiative focused on nurturing the next generation with Christlike character and values. We believe that instilling strong spiritual and moral foundations in children and youth prepares them to make positive contributions to society and their communities.

Program Details
  • Target Groups:

    Elementary, middle, and high school students.

  • Schedule:

    Wednesdays (evening) and Saturdays (afternoon)

Moral Injury/PTSD/Mental Health Spirituality Group
This program addresses the emotional and spiritual challenges faced by individuals dealing with moral injury, PTSD, and mental health issues. Led by a board-certified chaplain, the sessions offer a safe and supportive environment for healing.
Program Details
  • Confession
  • Forgiveness and Self-Forgiveness
  • Gratitude Practice
  • Self-Compassion
  • Building Resiliency
Register
Holistic Health Webinar
The Holistic Health Webinar is a transformative, interactive session aimed at promoting holistic well-being. The webinar is based on the Holistic Health Cycle of Health, which integrates spiritual, emotional, mental, and physical health to empower participants.
Program Details
  • Principles of self-care rooted in Christian values.
  • Techniques for managing physical, emotional, and spiritual health.
  • Practical tools for achieving balance and thriving in life.
