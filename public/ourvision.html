Welcome to Impact for Mission

Christian Therapeutic Ministry (CTM) exists to promote healing, restoration, and purpose through the power of faith and holistic care. Our vision is to see individuals, families, and communities thrive—spiritually, emotionally, and physically—by integrating biblical truth with practical support.

Our Mission Is Threefold

At CTM, we believe healing is not just individual—it is generational, global, and rooted in the Gospel.

Holistic Health Awareness & Teachings

We educate and equip individuals with faith-based tools for emotional, mental, and spiritual wellness through webinars, training sessions, and pastoral care.

Statement of Faith

1. Believe in Jesus.

We affirm that Jesus Christ is the only way to eternal life, based on John 3:16: "For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life.”

2. Bible

We believe in the authority and inerrancy of the Bible, as taught in 2 Timothy 3:16: "All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting, and training in righteousness.”

3. Holistic Health

We support a holistic approach to well-being that integrates physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual health. This aligns with 1 Thessalonians 5:23: "May God himself, the God of peace, sanctify you through and through. May your whole spirit, soul, and body be kept blameless at the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ."
